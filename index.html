How it works:
  • Toggle on — the mic listens continuously while you talk.
  • Speak in any supported language — English, Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi, Urdu, and more.
  • Watch your words appear live in the transcript box above.
  • Tap 📤 Send whenever you're done speaking — it goes through the same pipeline as typed messages.
  • ALEEM AI replies in the same language and speaks the response aloud.
  • Tap ⏹ Stop any time to cancel the AI's speech and go back to listening.
  • All messages, memories, and history are saved exactly like normal chat.
  • Toggle off to stop voice mode at any time.
🎯 Language Accuracy Tips
  • For the best speech recognition accuracy, manually select your spoken language from the dropdown before starting.
  • Do not rely on Auto mode for Marathi, Gujarati, or Urdu conversations.
  • Auto mode may misidentify languages on some browsers/devices and can reduce transcription accuracy.
  • If you primarily speak Hindi, Marathi, Gujarati, Urdu, or English, select that language explicitly.
  • The selected language only affects speech recognition accuracy — AI replies, memory, history, and multilingual responses continue to work normally.
  • If transcripts appear incorrect, switch to the matching language manually and try again.

ALEEM AI

Your Personal Knowledge Assistant

ALEEM AI is a state-of-the-art personal knowledge assistant built to make world-class artificial intelligence accessible to everyone. Created by Aleem Sudamaniya with a deep passion for education, technology, and cricket — ALEEM AI is more than a chatbot. It is an intelligent companion that grows with you, remembers what matters, answers questions across every domain, and inspires curiosity every single day.

🚀 Vision

To democratize access to AI-powered knowledge, making every person on the planet their own expert in any subject they choose to explore.

🌍 Mission

ALEEM AI exists to answer the questions that matter — from daily curiosities to deep academic knowledge — delivered with warmth, precision, and care.

⚡ Technology

Powered by Google Gemini, Groq, DeepSeek, and Cerebras AI — ALEEM AI uses state-of-the-art large language models for fast, accurate, nuanced responses across 12 personalities.

🏏 Expertise

From cricket statistics to quantum physics, history to humor, coding to creative writing — ALEEM AI is the most versatile knowledge assistant you'll ever use.

🔐 Privacy First

Your conversation history remains exclusively in your own browser. All AI requests are routed through a secure serverless function — your privacy is always paramount.

📱 Universal Compatibility

Runs on any device — desktop to mobile — as a fully responsive Progressive Web App that adapts perfectly to every screen size and platform.

🧠 Long-Term Memory

ALEEM AI automatically remembers your name, interests, goals, and favourites across sessions using Firestore — every conversation makes it smarter about you.
