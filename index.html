📖 About ALEEM AI

ALEEM AI is a state-of-the-art personal knowledge assistant designed to make world-class artificial intelligence accessible to everyone. Built with passion for education, cricket, and cutting-edge technology, ALEEM AI is more than a chatbot — it is an intelligent companion that grows with you and inspires curiosity every single day.

🌍 Mission Statement

Our mission is to democratize access to AI-powered knowledge, making every person on the planet their own expert in any subject they choose to explore. ALEEM AI exists to answer the questions that matter — from daily curiosities to deep academic knowledge — delivered with warmth and precision.

🔐 Privacy Information

  • Your conversations stay only in your own browser
  • No personal data is collected or stored on servers
  • AI requests are routed through a secure serverless proxy
  • The API key is never exposed to the browser or users
  • Voice data is processed locally on your device only

⚡ App Information

ALEEM AI is powered by Google Gemini AI with automatic model fallback for maximum reliability.
